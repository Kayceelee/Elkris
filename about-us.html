OUR Total- Health Vision For Africa

  • A true HealthCare model that saves vulnerable lives at the grassroots, (with specific respect to the continent of Africa) has been our top priority as a group of companies, for the past 15yrs. And in the past 4yrs alone, we have spent in excess of N450million of our corporate funds (without outside donations as at 2022), towards this noble social project. PreDiagnosis International - PDI (our flagship subsidiary) is at the driving seat in our pursuit of a lasting, transforming legacy on “how best to preserve health at the grassroots, in Africa”
  • Our Daily Pursuit - to see how many more people (who could have gone to an unnecessary early grave), who will continue to walk the streets of Nigeria, because PDI showed up!

Project 1million

We aim to:

  • Sensitize two (2) million adults across Nigerian, on the growing risk of “sudden Slump” by way of door-to-door BP Screening
  • Manage the personal BP health of 1million adult Nigerians (each month), through the use of “Elkris Bio Health-Plus”

ABOUT COBPA Initiative

  • An opportunity to join our national team of 50,000 prospective army of trained and certified “community BP Advocates” from all over Nigeria
  • A chance to help rescue lives in communities across Nigeria, who are going about with life threatening BP readings.
  • N350k to N900k monthly income - In addition to all of the above, COBPA Initiative was carefully designed to provide a platform for our advocates (you) to earn additional potential part-time income of N350k to N900k monthly, in the process!

THE BUSINESS OPPORTUNITY

  • Introducing the Elkris BioHealth-Plus Supplement! an all-in-one High Blood Pressure, Cholesterol & Diabetes control bioceutical!
  • Armed with EBP and a Personal BP Device, our army of COBPA can now join us the fight to save precious lives on the streets of Nigeria
